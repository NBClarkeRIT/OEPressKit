Description

Over Engineered is simulation strategy game that encourages construction of complex machines. Using limited space, money, and power; you must build complex contraptions to bring a ball from its spawn, to its goal. The longer each ball takes, the more money you make in return.

The Game Loop:

Each game begins with a ball dropping from a spawner. The player must place and connect different mechanical parts to guide the ball across the map. As the ball moves, it gains value from tricks, combos, and the time is has remained alive. The player must design their world to maximize these values to unlock more elaborate and sophisticated machines. With increasing ball values, the player will unlock new machines to expand upon their system, allowing the player to push their designs further than ever.

Gameplay That Rewards Creativity:

With future machines locked behind time milestons, the player must overcome time barriers to create something original. With 45+ parts, each new advancement propels the players machine further into Over-Engineered chaos

History

Over Engineered is originally a personal project meant to be developed and released over the course of a summer. Through support of friends & mentors, Over Engineered continued development the following year at Rochester Institute of Technology.
